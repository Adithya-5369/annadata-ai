=== INDIAN GOVERNMENT AGRICULTURE SCHEMES — COMPLETE GUIDE 2025 ===

--- PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi) ---
Launched: February 24, 2019
Benefit: Rs 6,000 per year in 3 instalments of Rs 2,000 every 4 months
Eligibility: All landholding farmer families with cultivable land, land size should not exceed 2 hectares
Payment: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account
Beneficiaries: 11.8 crore farmers nationwide
Total disbursed: Over Rs 3.45 lakh crore
How to apply: Register at pmkisan.gov.in or nearest CSC center
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, bank account details, land ownership records
Helpline: 011-24300606
Website: pmkisan.gov.in

--- PRADHAN MANTRI FASAL BIMA YOJANA (PMFBY) ---
Launched: 2016
Purpose: Crop insurance against natural calamities, pests, diseases
Premium rates: 2% for Kharif crops, 1.5% for Rabi crops, 5% for horticulture
Coverage: Pre-sowing to post-harvest losses including prevented sowing
Claims disbursed: Over Rs 1.5 lakh crore to farmers
How to apply: Through bank or insurance company before crop season starts
Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops
Website: pmfby.gov.in

--- SOIL HEALTH CARD SCHEME ---
Purpose: Free soil testing and customized fertilizer recommendations
Benefit: Soil health cards with specific nutrient recommendations per crop
Cards distributed: Over 25 crore soil health cards issued
Eligibility: All farmers across India irrespective of land size
How to apply: Contact nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or agriculture department
Frequency: Soil testing done every 2 years
Website: soilhealth.dac.gov.in

--- KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC) ---
Purpose: Short term credit for agriculture and allied activities
Interest rate: 4% per annum with government interest subvention
Loan limit: Up to Rs 3 lakh at 7% interest with timely repayment
Flexible repayment tied to crop season
How to apply: Nearest bank branch with land ownership documents and Aadhaar
Eligibility: All farmers, sharecroppers, tenant farmers

--- PM KRISHI SINCHAI YOJANA (PMKSY) ---
Full form: Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana
Purpose: Irrigation infrastructure and water use efficiency
Subsidy: 55% for small and marginal farmers, 45% for others on micro irrigation
Focus: Per Drop More Crop initiative
Coverage: Over 29 lakh hectares irrigated since 2016
Benefit: Drip and sprinkler irrigation systems at subsidized rates

--- e-NAM (NATIONAL AGRICULTURE MARKET) ---
Purpose: Online trading platform for agricultural produce
Markets linked: 1,389 mandis across India
Registered farmers: Over 1.8 crore
Benefit: Farmers can sell produce online at better prices, greater transparency, reduced middlemen
Website: enam.gov.in

--- RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (RKVY) / PM-RKVY ---
Purpose: Holistic development of agriculture sector
Benefit: Funds for infrastructure, mechanization, storage, processing
How to access: Through state agriculture department
Rationalized in October 2024 as PM-RKVY under two umbrella schemes

--- PARAMPARAGAT KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (PKVY) ---
Purpose: Promote organic farming and sustainable agriculture
Benefit: Rs 50,000 per hectare for 3 years for organic practices
Eligibility: Minimum 20 farmers over 50-acre cluster
Includes: Certification support and market linkage for organic produce

--- NATIONAL FOOD SECURITY MISSION (NFSM) ---
Purpose: Increase production of rice, wheat, pulses, oilseeds, maize
Benefit: Subsidized seeds, fertilizers, farm equipment
Coverage: Major food grain producing states across India

--- AGRICULTURE INFRASTRUCTURE FUND (AIF) ---
Purpose: Post harvest management and agriculture logistics infrastructure
Loan: Up to Rs 2 crore with 3% interest subvention
Eligible: Farmers, FPOs, cooperatives, agri entrepreneurs
For: Cold storage, warehouses, processing units

--- SUB-MISSION ON AGRICULTURAL MECHANIZATION (SMAM) ---
Purpose: Encourage use of modern farm machinery
Benefit: Subsidies on tractors, harvesters, seeders, threshers
Eligible: Small and marginal farmers
How to apply: State agriculture department

--- NATIONAL MISSION ON NATURAL FARMING (NMNF) ---
Launched: November 25, 2024
Total outlay: Rs 2,481 crore till 2025-26
Purpose: Promote chemical-free farming based on traditional knowledge
Benefit: Training, support, financial aid for natural farming practices
Goal: Increase area under natural farming by 50% by 2025
Reduces: Input costs, soil degradation, health risks from fertilizers

--- NATIONAL BEEKEEPING AND HONEY MISSION (NBHM) ---
Launched: 2020
Purpose: Promote scientific beekeeping and honey production
Benefit: Training, equipment, financial assistance to beekeepers
Achievement: Honey production increased from 12,000 MT to 20,000 MT

--- NATIONAL MISSION ON EDIBLE OILS - OILSEEDS (NMEO-OILSEEDS) ---
Approved: October 3, 2024
Outlay: Rs 10,103 crore
Purpose: Reduce India's dependence on imported edible oils
Benefit: Incentives to grow oilseeds, support for processing mills

--- KRISHONNATI YOJANA (KY) ---
Purpose: Umbrella scheme covering multiple agriculture missions
Rationalized October 2024 along with PM-RKVY
Covers: Crop development, horticulture, soil health, rainfed farming

--- DIGITAL AGRICULTURE MISSION ---
Outlay: Rs 2,817 crore
Purpose: Use technology for improving farmers lives
Based on Digital Public Infrastructure framework
Includes: AgriStack digital identity for farmers, satellite monitoring

--- MINIMUM SUPPORT PRICE (MSP) ---
Purpose: Ensure minimum guaranteed price for crops
Crops covered: 23 crops including paddy, wheat, pulses, oilseeds, cotton
2025 MSP highlights: Copra MSP increased by 121% since 2014
Set by: Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices (CACP)

--- UREA SUBSIDY SCHEME ---
Purpose: Ensure subsidized urea availability to farmers
DAP Special Package: Extended till further orders from January 1, 2025
DAP subsidy: Rs 3,500 per MT beyond NBS subsidy
Benefit: Affordable fertilizers for all farmers across India

--- AGRICULTURAL AND PROCESSED FOOD PRODUCTS EXPORT (APEDA) ---
Purpose: Increase export of agricultural and processed food products
Benefit: Financial and logistical support for agricultural exports
Helps farmers access global markets

--- KRISHI UDAAN 2.0 ---
Revised: 2025
Purpose: Strengthen market access for perishable and high-value crops
Benefit: Freight subsidies especially for Northeast and remote region farmers
Includes: Cold chain infrastructure grants, e-NAM expansion

--- PRADHAN MANTRI RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (PM-RKVY) ---
Rationalized: October 3, 2024
Purpose: Foster India's agricultural economy
Benefit: Grants for farm mechanization, construction, infrastructure
How to apply: Nearest agriculture department with Aadhaar and land papers

--- CROP SCIENCE FOR FOOD AND NUTRITIONAL SECURITY ---
Outlay: Rs 3,979 crore
Purpose: Prepare farmers for climate resilience, food security by 2047
Includes: Research on insects, microbes, pollinators, climate-resistant varieties

--- SUSTAINABLE LIVESTOCK HEALTH AND PRODUCTION ---
Outlay: Rs 1,702 crore
Purpose: Increase farmer income from livestock and dairy
Benefit: Disease control, veterinary support, dairy infrastructure

--- HOW TO APPLY FOR MOST SCHEMES ---
Step 1: Visit nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or agriculture department
Step 2: Carry Aadhaar card, bank passbook, land records
Step 3: Register on respective scheme portal
Step 4: Benefits transferred directly via DBT to bank account
Helpline for agriculture queries: Kisan Call Center 1800-180-1551 (toll free)
PM-KISAN Helpline: 011-24300606
General agriculture helpline: 1800-180-1551
